Me in a Few Sentences

I'm Pete. Professionally, I'm the Manager of Customer Education & Certification at AtScale, where I build strategies to maximize user success. Beyond the office, I'm a devoted family man, a home automation tinkerer, and an enthusiast for local AI.

With a background in mathematics and philosophy, I love breaking down complex systems, optimizing workflows with Python and LLMs, and building elegant, functional solutions to modern problems.

Books That Have Shaped Me

  • Thinking, Fast and Slow by Daniel Kahneman
    Fundamentally changed how I approach training audiences and behavioral design.
  • Atomic Habits by James Clear
    A masterful guide on compounding systems over goals.
  • Whiplash by Joi Ito & Jeff Howe
    Brilliant insights into navigating rapid technological change and complex systems.
  • To Kill a Mockingbird by Harper Lee
    The author actually shares my birthday! Reading this in school made me want to be a lawyer for a while.
  • On the Shortness of Life by Seneca
    A foundational Stoic text that reminds me to be intentional and purposeful with my time.
  • The Myth of Sisyphus & The Stranger by Albert Camus
    Existential reads that deeply challenged and shaped my philosophical perspective.
